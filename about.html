The founder story

Brand Here isn't a new agency. It's a second chapter.

Alton Nguyen founded Brand Here in January 2015, building it into an integrated marketing communications agency focused on healthcare, pharmaceutical and corporate branding. Over five years, the agency delivered 80+ projects for 50+ corporate clients, including regional work with organizations in Thailand and Hong Kong.

In November 2020, he stepped away from the agency to go in-house at multinational pharmaceutical companies — first supporting commercialization for major GI and nutrition brands, then leading a company's first e-commerce and digital transformation initiative, and currently serving as a senior commercial leader managing a significant annual portfolio and 30+ brands as part of a Vietnam Management Team, delivering four consecutive years above the Annual Operating Plan.

That five-year detour wasn't a pause from building Brand Here — it was the missing piece. Most agency founders never sit on the client side long enough to feel the weight of a real P&L. Alton has now done both: built the agency, and run the budget multinationals actually hold their marketing leaders accountable for.

That's why Brand Here is restarting — this time with a second pillar: AI solutions, applied the way an operator would apply them, not the way a typical agency pitches them.

Why now

The market doesn't need another AI agency. There are already many.

What's harder to find is someone who has actually run both sides — agency delivery and in-house commercial ownership of a multi-million-dollar portfolio across multinational pharma — while also staying close to the founders and business owners who don't have a corporate infrastructure behind them.

Not "what's technically possible," but "what will actually survive contact with a real P&L, a real compliance process, and a real leadership team" — whether that team is a regional management board or a founder's kitchen table.

That's the specific gap Brand Here is built to fill.

The Bridge philosophy

Our identity reflects what we do: we bridge two things that are often disconnected — ambitious ideas and the operational reality of executing them.

Current State → Transformation → Future State. That's not just a tagline. It's the actual sequence of every engagement.

Where Brand Here came from

Over 8 years, Brand Here worked across three disciplines — Communication (PR, events, activation), Content (video, photography, articles), and Creative (branding, packaging, design) — for organizations including Roche, AIA, Nestlé, Herbalife, Microsoft, Toshiba, Vinpearl, Dragon Capital, Subway, Oreo, and Western Sydney University, across healthcare, finance, technology, education, F&B and real estate.

That range — blue-chip clients, multiple industries, both strategic and hands-on execution — is the foundation this next chapter builds on.
