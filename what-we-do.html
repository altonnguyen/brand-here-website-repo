What we do

Three ways we bridge strategy and execution.

Strategy Consulting

Strategy built by someone who's had to live with the consequences of it.

Most strategy advice comes from people who present a plan and leave before it's tested. Alton built strategy for 15+ years while also being the one responsible for whether it worked — first as an agency founder, then as a commercial leader managing multi-million-dollar portfolios inside multinational pharma.

What this includes:

  • Market positioning and brand strategy
  • Business model and go-to-market planning

AI Transformation

AI for the decisions only you can make — whether you run a division or the whole company.

We don't build chatbots or run automation pilots that sit in a drawer. We build AI-powered decision support for the people actually accountable for outcomes — C-suite executives inside large organizations, and owners running their own business — grounded in how decisions are really made, because that's where Alton spent 15+ years operating, both as a founder and inside multinational leadership teams.

What this includes:

  • Executive decision-support dashboards (market, competitor, portfolio intelligence)
  • Practical AI adoption roadmaps for business owners who need results, not a lab experiment
  • AI-assisted strategic planning and scenario analysis, scaled to the size of the organization

Executive Branding

Your track record is the asset. We help you communicate it.

Many executives have real operating experience but no public presence that reflects it. Alton's own restart of Brand Here is a live example: building a public presence based on what he's actually done, not a generic thought-leadership template.

What this includes:

  • Personal brand strategy for founders/executives
  • Content and thought leadership planning
