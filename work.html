Rebuilding Brand Here with AI

Situation: After 5+ years away building commercial experience inside multinational pharma, Alton decided to restart Brand Here — this time adding AI as a core capability. Rather than hiring a strategy consultant or a branding agency, he used AI directly, end-to-end.

Approach: Used AI to stress-test market positioning, iterate through logo concepts (catching a visual flaw before it shipped), draft and refine the entire website in direct collaboration, and plan the technical deployment — without a developer on staff.

Result: The website you're reading right now — logo, copy, structure and deployment — was built through this exact process. It's not a hypothetical demonstration of what AI can do; it's the direct output of it.

What this demonstrates: This is what "AI for leadership decision-making" looks like in practice — a founder using AI as a genuine thinking partner for high-stakes decisions, the same way Brand Here now offers this to clients.
